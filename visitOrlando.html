Have a perfect family holiday in Orlando with 360 Travels



Orlando, Florida, is the dream destination for the family holiday of a lifetime. The legendary Sunshine State city is home to a magical array of the world's best theme parks. From high-looping roller coasters to immersive movie magic, it's a fun-packed thrill ride from dawn until dusk. Not that you need an extra incentive, but 360 Travels are now the Holiday Prize Partner for Ant & Dec's Saturday Night Takeaway, too...

Universal Orlando Resort



When one theme park just isn't enough, stay at the resort that has three. Fancy a plunge down a 125ft drop water slide? Grab your swimsuits for Universal's Volcano Bay water theme park. Then, immerse yourself in the next generation of blockbuster entertainment at Universal Studios Florida before journeying through legendary worlds of incredible heroes at Universal' Islands of Adventure. Whatever your accommodation needs are, eight hotel options range from premium glitz at the Hard Rock Hotel®, to fresh and fun value at Universal's Endless Summer Resort.

Ramada Plaza Resort and Suites International Drive



This is just the spot for comfortable, cheerful accommodation and great value for money. The Ramada Plaza Resort enjoys an excellent location on Orlando's famous International Drive and the hotel provides a free daily shuttle service to the city's main attractions. A complimentary continental breakfast is on offer, so the whole family can fuel up on cereal, fruit and waffles before a big day of adventuring. There are many more restaurants within walking distance, too, as well as shops galore at Pointe Orlando and The Florida Mall. Our top tip is to book a club-level suite for exclusive access to the rooftop pool, bar and sundeck. Plus, drinks are unlimited on the club level from 12pm to 8pm every day.

Rosen Shingle Creek



The Rosen Shingle Creek brings five-star elegance to Orlando's Universal Boulevard. As well as your proximity to the city's many theme parks, you'll find plenty of diversions at home, with four pools and a spa available for adults who need to unwind (the Himalayan salt stone massage should do the trick). Plus, golfers will be enamoured with the Shingle Creek Golf Club's award-winning course, expertly designed to be both challenging and entertaining. Dining options abound, too, from a morning coffee and pastry at Smooth Java, to the AAA Four Diamond Italian bistro, Cala Bella. If you do visit the latter, we recommend the superb rock lobster cacio e pepe.

Waldorf Astoria Orlando



The Waldorf Astoria name assures sophisticated luxury, and this Florida location does not disappoint. All five-star rooms and suites deliver plush furnishings, stylish warm décor and Italian marble bathrooms. Your gastronomic choices here are bountiful, but treating yourself to a filet mignon at the lauded Bull & Bear steakhouse is a decision you won't regret. While away your days in a private cabana by one of two spacious pools or practise your long game on the resort's 18-hole golf course. If you have little ones to entertain, daily poolside lawn games and scavenger hunts will help. You won't need to go far for theme park frivolity, either, as Walt Disney World borders the hotel on three sides.

Caribe Royale Orlando



Swaying palms adorn the grounds of this impressive contemporary resort. Ideal for those who love an active holiday, the Caribe Royale Orlando has a two-storey fitness centre, tennis courts, basketball courts and a 1.5-mile walking and running trail. All that activity should be rewarded and a cocktail at the Rum bar is the way to do it - we'd go for a Rum Negroni 16 with Black Tot, sweet vermouth and vanilla, along with some Cuban-style tapas. Weekend nights are particularly special here, with poolside 'Movies under the Stars' screenings of family-friendly films. Naturally, Orlando's theme parks are an easy distance away by shuttle or car.

Drury Plaza Hotel Orlando



Located in the Disney Springs Resort area, the Drury Plaza Hotel Orlando boasts mesmerising views of Lake Buena Vista. Florida's trademark sun means temperatures can get high, but cooling off is a breeze with a saltwater pool, whirlpool and splash pad at your disposal. Take advantage of the complimentary shuttle service to spend joyful days at Disney's Magic Kingdom Park, Hollywood Studios and EPCOT. This is an official Walt Disney World hotel, so you'll benefit from early entrance when you get there. Breakfast is also free, but familiar favourites Starbucks and Pizza Hut Express are at the ready to provide extra sustenance. Oh, and there's also the 5:30 Kickback, where guests can enjoy a rotating menu of complimentary drinks and hot snacks every night between 5.30-7.30pm (up to three alcoholic or non-alcoholic drinks).

CoCo Key Water Park & Resort



Aquatic fun is the theme every day at this colourful resort. The on-site water park is the star of the show, with 14 slides and rides, plus a 6,500sqft arcade. While the kids are having a whale of a time, you can take it easy at the Wet Rooster Tiki Bar with a well-earned cocktail. Dining options are simple and family-friendly here, with a hot buffet breakfast at Tradewinds restaurant and burgers, pizzas and more at the Callaloo Grill. The guest rooms are cosy, light and outfitted with a 55in flatscreen TV - great for chilling out after a busy day. Situated on International Drive, this hotel is the perfect base from which to enjoy the city.
